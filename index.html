AN INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY

OUR FESTIVAL IS THE OCCASION TO CONNECT WITH INDIE MOVIE LOVERS LIKE YOU

The Watch it Outside Film Festival is an American film festival that first took place in the city of Chicago in 2015. With an average of 15,000 attendees each year, it is one of the largest independent film festivals in the United States.

Held during the summer in the biggest cities in the US, in Park City, the festival is a showcase for new work from American and international independent filmmakers. The festival comprises sections for American and international dramatic and documentary films.
